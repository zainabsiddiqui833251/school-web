about


The School is purpose-built on 28 acres of land and caters more than 2500 students with state- of-the-art laboratories, playgrounds, libraries and audio visual activity rooms which give immense exposure to the students and the teachers alike. The School follows the Matriculation system of education and since inception has been producing excellent results. Even during the lockdown of 2020-2021 the School’s faculty members have let no stone unturned to impart quality education through interactive online sessions. The Pakistan National Commission for UNESCO has certified that x-y-z School is a member of the UNESCO ASSOCIATED SCHOOL PROJECT NETWORK in Education for International co-operation and peace. Also, the Middle Section of the School is the proud winner of British Council’s International School Award for the years 2019 to 2022. x-y-z Public School has vast and strong alumni network consisting of more than ten thousand talented alumni serving worldwide in renowned national and international organizations. Moreover, the School takes pride in producing graduates that are serving as Government Officials in different regions of Pakistan. Finally, I would like to thank and appreciate the valuable guardians who trust us with their child’s education and encourage all the first time visitors to go through the website and visit the campus to gain further information about x-y-z Public School situated at x-y-z city,country

sections in school

prepatory

Welcome to our Preparatory Section, dedicated to the education of children aged 3.5 years and above. Here, we focus on fostering a nurturing environment that encourages curiosity and creativity. Our qualified educators employ innovative teaching methods to lay a strong foundation for lifelong learning. Explore a vibrant learning space designed to cater to the unique needs of our youngest learners, providing them with the tools they need to embark on their educational journey

primary

In the Primary Section, we pride ourselves on offering exclusive attention to students in classes 1-5. Our dedicated team of educators is committed to building a solid academic foundation while nurturing the holistic development of each child. We recognize the crucial early years of education and tailor our curriculum to instill a love for learning. With a focus on interactive lessons and personalized support, we aim to empower students to become confident, critical thinkers who are well-prepared for the challenges ahead.

secondary

Prepare for excellence in our Middle and Secondary Section, designed to guide students through classes 6-10. Here, we go beyond academics to cultivate well-rounded individuals. Our experienced faculty is dedicated to providing a challenging yet supportive environment, emphasizing critical thinking, collaboration, and problem-solving skills.

higher secondary

As we prepare students for the rigors of higher education, our focus remains on holistic development, ensuring they emerge not only academically proficient but also equipped with the skills necessary to thrive in an ever-changing world. Our commitment is to provide best results in Secondary School Certificates that make our students outshine.

  • Damage to school property. Irregularity in attendance without valid reason.

  • Using unfair means during tests or examinations.

  • Poor performance in studies.

  • Failing two consecutive years in the same class.

  • Fee default of two months and above.

  • attendance less than 75%.
